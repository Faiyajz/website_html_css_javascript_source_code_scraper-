Overview

Epione is a scientifically oriented innovation with creation led Indian multinational Pharmaceutical Company. The word Epione has its own identity. As per Greek mythology Epione means the Goddess of soothing of pain. Epione Pharma arriving on the horizon of Indian Pharmaceutical industry with an outlook to bring innovative dosage formulations suited for masses suffering from various health problems. We take utmost care to understand the needs of our customers. This enables us to develop innovative quality products at an affordable cost. Right from research to formulation development, the company is committed to keep everything world-class yet affordable for all.

With its auxillary network and representative offices, Epione has bookmarked its presence in Maharashtra, Goa, Gujarat, Chattisgarh with Export in Kenya, US.

Our organic and consistent growth attributed to the experienced team of professionals with collective work experience of more than 50 years in the healthcare industry.

Epione aspires to be customer-centric with a view to cater the needs of various specialties of medical fraternity in India. Epione committed towards a healthier and happier world, we strive to provide unique formulations with quality & affordability that accessible to all.
